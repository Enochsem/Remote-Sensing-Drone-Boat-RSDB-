About RSDB

The Remote Sensing Drone Boat, was built ,

  • to measure some physicochemical properties of water - pH, turbidity,temperature,electrical conductivity - suitable for aquaculture.
  • to provide real-time analysis of the sensor data and recommend appropriate corrective measures.
  • to build a system that can transport and collect data efficiently by means of air or water for better surveillance.
  • to design a mobile and low cost water quality checker.
  • to be able to carry out water quality analysis at remote locations.
  • to eradicate errors and accidents that take place when the data is collected manually.
