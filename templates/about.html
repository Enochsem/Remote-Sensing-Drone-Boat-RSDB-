OBJECTIVES OF RSDB

  1. To measure some physiochemical properties of water - pH, turbidity,temperature,electrical conductivity - suitable for aquaculture.

  2. To provide real-time analysis of the sensor data and recommend appropriate corrective measures.

  3. To build a system that can transport and collect data efficiently by means of air or water for better surveillance.

  4. To design a mobile and low cost water quality checker.

  5. To be able to carry out water quality analysis at remote locations.

  6. To eradicate errors and accidents that take place when the data is collected manually.
