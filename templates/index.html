Remote Sensing Made Easy


PURPOSE OF RSDB

1 To measure some physiochemical properties of water - pH, turbidity,temperature,electrical conductivity - suitable for aquaculture.
2 To provide real-time analysis of the sensor data and recommend appropriate corrective measures.
3 To be able to carry out water quality analysis at remote locations
4 To design a mobile and low cost water quality checker.
